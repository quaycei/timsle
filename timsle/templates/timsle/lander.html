Our challenge was mental health.
Our solution is well-being.






We set out in the beginning to build a tool for those among us suffering from mental illnesses. Through our journey, we began to see that the nature of the problem we're working on isn't just mental health - it's health.

As we continue to build the Timsle platform, we’re growing it into something for everyone that could use . It might be for someone who wants to eat healthier, improve their study habits, or make new friends. Our commitment is to making the platform as helpful to you as possible, so that anyone, with a mental illness or not, can use Timsle to help them improve their lifestyle.
